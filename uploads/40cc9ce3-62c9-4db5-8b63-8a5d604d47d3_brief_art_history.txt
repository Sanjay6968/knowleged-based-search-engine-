
Title: A Brief Overview of Art History

Art is the creative expression of human thoughts and emotions, evolving through ages:

- **Prehistoric Art:** Early cave paintings and carvings showing daily life and rituals.
- **Ancient Art:** Egyptian, Greek, and Roman works focused on gods, rulers, and ideal human forms.
- **Medieval Art:** Religious themes dominated with stained glass and illuminated manuscripts.
- **Renaissance:** Realism, perspective, and humanism flourished through artists like da Vinci and Michelangelo.
- **Modern Art:** Movements like Impressionism and Cubism broke traditional rules.
- **Contemporary Art:** Blends technology, emotion, and social themes, exploring limitless creativity.
